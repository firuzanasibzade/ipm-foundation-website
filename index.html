About Us

Our Mission

The IPM Foundation is dedicated to providing access to project management knowledge, education, and resources to underserved communities and non-profit organizations. Our mission is to empower individuals by equipping them with the skills needed to manage successful projects and drive positive change in their communities through effective project management practices.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a world where project management skills are accessible to all, especially in education, healthcare, and non-profits. Through the IPM Foundation, we aim to enable individuals and organizations to thrive, fostering sustainable development and empowering the next generation of project managers.

Why We Set Up the IPM Foundation

The IPM Foundation was established to extend the mission of the Institute of Project Management (IPM) beyond professional training and certifications. Project management is a key skill for success, but it is often not accessible to those who need it most. The Foundation allows us to give back by providing knowledge and support to those in need, helping them achieve better project outcomes and have a meaningful impact on their communities.

Our Goals

  • Provide Access to Project Management Education: Offering free or subsidized training to underserved communities to equip them with valuable project management skills.
  • Support Non-Profits and Social Enterprises: Assisting non-profits by improving their project management capabilities, ensuring they can better serve their communities.
  • Create Volunteer and Mentorship Opportunities: Engaging IPM professionals and volunteers to support emerging project managers and guide non-profits in their project efforts.
  • Raise Awareness of Project Management's Value: Promoting the importance of project management and its potential to drive success in various sectors, such as education and healthcare.
